Keto 
The Keto Diet is a low carb high fat diet that involves drastically reducing your carbohydrate intake and
       replacing it with fat. The reduction in carbs forces the body into a metabolic state of ketosis. While in the
       state of ketosis the body becomes efficient at burning fat for energy. It also transforms fat into ketones in
       the liver,which can supply energy for the brain.
